Game Info

Description: Chase the wind, shoot the breeze and do much more in “All is Vanity,” Xenizo Games’ exciting dual-stick, isometric action/arcade game that transports players to a world of Middle Eastern madness.

Follow the royal teacher Qoheleth and his allies as they defend “everything under the sun” and teach Shishaq a lesson. Will our heroes use their wind-control powers wisely, or will they succumb to the temptations of success, luxury and fame?
